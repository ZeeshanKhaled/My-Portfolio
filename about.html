Who I Am

Student, developer and creative builder

I am currently studying Computer Science and AI, with a strong interest in web development, interactive design and practical technology. I enjoy projects that let me think logically while still giving me space to be creative with layout, visuals and user experience.

My experience in retail, customer service and IT support has also helped me build confidence in communication, teamwork, organisation and problem-solving.

What I Build

Websites, games and product ideas

I build front-end projects using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, especially when the project has a strong visual identity or interactive element. My work includes portfolio pages, university websites, browser games and product-style concepts such as SunSync.

I care about making projects feel polished, so I pay attention to spacing, responsiveness, accessibility, colour choices and small details that improve the user experience.

Approach

Iterative, practical and detail-focused

I usually start by breaking a project into smaller parts, then build and refine each section until it feels right. I am comfortable testing ideas, improving layouts, fixing issues and adjusting the design based on how the final product looks and works.

My approach mixes technical thinking with design awareness: I care about whether something works, but also whether it is easy to understand and enjoyable to use.

Interests

Games, sport and digital creativity

I enjoy game-inspired interfaces, browser games, AI concepts, product ideas and creative digital projects. Outside of tech, I enjoy playing video games and table tennis, where I have taken on leadership by captaining a team, organising try-outs and helping others improve through feedback.

Future Direction

Growing toward software, AI and product design

I want to keep developing my skills in software development, AI concepts, front-end design and project building. I am especially interested in opportunities where I can combine problem-solving, creativity and communication while continuing to build a portfolio with personality.
